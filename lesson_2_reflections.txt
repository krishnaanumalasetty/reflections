what happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?

Initialize -> Creates a .git where it holds all the history files etc. Nothing is committed or tracked.


How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?

Staging area is the intermediate area where the files are tracked. Working Directory files are not tracked.
